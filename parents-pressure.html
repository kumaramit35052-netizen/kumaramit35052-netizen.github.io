Parents Pressure vs Student Interest

Parents Pressure vs Student Interest ek aisa topic hai jo har Indian student ke life me kabhi na kabhi zaroor aata hai. Is issue me zyada tar cases me parents bhi galat nahi hote aur students bhi galat nahi hote. Problem sirf understanding ki hoti hai.

Parents apne experience ke basis par sochte hain. Unhone apne time me jo dekha, suna ya jhela hota hai, unhe wahi future ke liye safest lagta hai. Isliye jab baat padhai aur career ki aati hai, to unka wahi purana mindset activate ho jata hai — “jo main keh raha hoon wahi sahi hai”.

Bahut se parents apne adhoore sapne apne bachchon ke through poore karna chahte hain. Doctor, Engineer, IAS — yeh sab dreams parents ke liye respect aur security ka symbol hote hain. Lekin yahin par sabse badi problem start hoti hai, jab bachche ka interest bilkul alag hota hai.

Student ka interest ignore kar dena parents ki sabse badi galti hoti hai. Kyuki jisme interest nahi hota, wo cheez bojh ban jaati hai. Aur jisme interest hota hai, usi me insaan apna 100% deta hai.

Main Indian parents se respectfully kehna chahta hoon — aap apne bachchon ko force mat kariye. Thoda unki baat bhi suniye. Agar bachcha kisi cheez me genuinely interested hai, to usse ek mauka zaroor dijiye.

Ek simple example dekhiye — jab ghar me koi favourite dish banti hai, to sabse pehle usi par dhyaan jata hai. Lekin jab karela banta hai, to zyada log usse avoid karte hain. Kyun? Kyuki interest nahi hota. Bilkul wahi cheez career ke saath bhi hoti hai.

Jis field me interest hota hai, wahi kaam achhe se hota hai. Chahe wo Science ho, Commerce ho, Humanities ho, Sports ho, Music ho ya Business ho. Interest + Mehnat = Success.

Students ke liye bhi ek message hai. Sirf bol dena kaafi nahi hota ki “mujhe yeh karna hai”. Students ko apne parents ko proof dena padta hai. Skills develop karo, results lao, discipline dikhao.

Parents aur students ke beech fight nahi, discussion honi chahiye. Career ka decision ek din me nahi hota. Time lo, explore karo, samjho aur phir decide karo.

Final baat — Parents ka experience important hai, aur students ka interest bhi utna hi important hai. Jab dono mil jaate hain, tab best future banta hai.
