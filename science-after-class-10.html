Class 10 Ke Baad Science Lena Sahi Hai Ya Nahi?

Dekho, Class 10 ke baad Science lena ya na lena sirf aur sirf tum par depend karta hai. Koi bhi stream bekaar nahi hoti — Science, Commerce aur Humanities, teeno apni jagah best hain.

Lekin main yeh bhi maanta hoon ki zyadatar parents ko lagta hai Science subject sabse best hai. Unhe lagta hai ki agar bachcha Science le lega, to wo kuch na kuch zaroor kar lega. Lekin sach yeh hai ki sirf Science lene se success guarantee nahi hoti.

Stream wahi sahi hoti hai jisme tumhara interest ho. Jaise jab hum kahin ghoomne jaate hain, to wahan maza isliye aata hai kyonki wahan humara interest hota hai. Bilkul waise hi, padhai bhi tabhi easy lagti hai jab subject pasand ho.

Main tumhe ek real example deta hoon. Mera ek friend tha jiska mann Commerce lene ka tha, lekin uske papa chahte the ki wo Biology le. Papa ke pressure me usne Class 11th me Biology le li.

Hamare school me board registration se pehle 2 mahine padhai karwa ke ek test hota hai, taaki yeh pata chal sake ki student subject handle kar pa raha hai ya nahi. Us exam me sabke achhe marks aaye, lekin mera woh friend fail ho gaya.

Maine usse pucha, “bhai padha nahi tha kya?” To usne kaha, “Science mujhe pasand hi nahi, mujhe Commerce achha lagta hai.”

Tab maine usse bola, “phir Science li hi kyun?” Usne kaha, “Papa force kar rahe the.”

Maine usse yahi kaha jo main aaj tumse keh raha hoon — jisme interest ho, wahi karo. Usne apne parents ko samjhaya, aur thodi mushkil ke baad Commerce le li.

November me half-yearly exam hua aur usne top kiya, jabki uska 2 mahine ka backlog bhi tha. Sirf isliye, kyonki us subject me uska interest tha.

Is example se yeh bhi samajh aata hai ki sirf marks ya dusron ki success dekh kar decision lena aaj ke time me sabse badi galti ho sakti hai. Har student alag hota hai, har student ki capacity, interest aur soch alag hoti hai.

Bahut se students Science isliye le lete hain kyonki unhe lagta hai ki agar Science nahi li to log kya kahenge, rishtedaar kya bolenge, ya phir future secure nahi hoga. Lekin sach yeh hai ki future tabhi secure hota hai jab tum apni field me genuinely mehnat kar pao.

Aaj ke time me sirf doctor ya engineer banna hi success nahi hai. Commerce aur Humanities me bhi CA, CS, Economics, Law, Teaching, UPSC, Design jaise bahut achhe career options available hain. Bas farq itna hai ki log abhi bhi purani soch me phase hue hain.

Agar tumhe Science pasand hai, concepts samajhne me maza aata hai, aur thoda struggle karne ke liye ready ho, to Science lena bilkul sahi decision ho sakta hai. Lekin agar sirf pressure ki wajah se Science le rahe ho, to aage chal kar wahi subject tumhare liye tension aur stress ban sakta hai.

Isliye final decision lene se pehle khud se honestly pucho — “kya mujhe is subject ke saath 2 saal bitane me interest aayega?” Agar jawab haan hai, to bina dare aage badho. Aur agar jawab nahi hai, to apne parents se shanti se baat karo aur apni baat unhe samjhao.
