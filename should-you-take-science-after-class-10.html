Class 10 Ke Baad Science Lena Sahi Hai Ya Nahi?

Dekho, Class 10 ke baad Science lena ya na lena sirf aur sirf tum par depend karta hai. Koi bhi stream bekaar nahi hoti — Science, Commerce aur Humanities, teeno apni jagah best hain.

Lekin main yeh bhi maanta hoon ki zyadatar parents ko lagta hai Science subject sabse best hai. Unhe lagta hai ki agar bachcha Science le lega, to wo kuch na kuch zaroor kar lega. Lekin sach yeh hai ki sirf Science lene se success guarantee nahi hoti.

Stream wahi sahi hoti hai jisme tumhara interest ho. Jaise jab hum kahin ghoomne jaate hain, to wahan maza isliye aata hai kyonki wahan humara interest hota hai. Bilkul waise hi, padhai bhi tabhi easy lagti hai jab subject pasand ho.

Main tumhe ek real example deta hoon. Mera ek friend tha jiska mann Commerce lene ka tha, lekin uske papa chahte the ki wo Biology le. Papa ke pressure me usne Class 11th me Biology le li.

Hamare school me board registration se pehle 2 mahine padhai karwa ke ek test hota hai, taaki yeh pata chal sake ki student subject handle kar pa raha hai ya nahi. Us exam me sabke achhe marks aaye, lekin mera woh friend fail ho gaya.

Maine usse pucha, “bhai padha nahi tha kya?” To usne kaha, “Science mujhe pasand hi nahi, mujhe Commerce achha lagta hai.” Tab maine usse bola, “phir Science li hi kyun?” Usne kaha, “Papa force kar rahe the.”

Maine usse yahi kaha jo main aaj tumse keh raha hoon — jisme interest ho, wahi karo. Usne apne parents ko samjhaya, aur thodi mushkil ke baad Commerce le li.

November me half-yearly exam hua aur usne top kiya, jabki uska 2 mahine ka backlog bhi tha. Sirf isliye, kyonki us subject me uska interest tha.

Is example se ek baat bilkul clear ho jaati hai — interest ke bina padhai bojh ban jaati hai, aur interest ke saath padhai result dene lagti hai. Agar tum Science sirf isliye le rahe ho kyonki society ya parents keh rahe hain, to thoda ruk kar socho.

Aaj ke time me Commerce aur Humanities me bhi bahut achhe career options hain. CA, CS, Economics, Law, Design, Journalism, UPSC — yeh sab kisi bhi angle se Science se kam nahi hain. Bas zaroorat hai apni strength pehchaanne ki.

Parents galat nahi hote, wo bas secure future chahte hain. Isliye unhe gusse se nahi, shanti aur examples ke saath samjhana chahiye. Jab parents dekhenge ki tum apne decision ko lekar serious ho, to wo zaroor samjhenge.

Yaad rakho, success stream se nahi, mehnat aur consistency se aati hai. Jo student apne interest ke saath padhai karta hai, wo kisi bhi field me aage badh sakta hai.
